Steps to Make Sandwich

  1. Get ingredients: Bread, peanut butter and jelly.
  2. Lay out 2 pieces of bread.
  3. Grab a knife.
  4. Spread peanut butter on one side of bread.
  5. Spread jelly on the other side of bread.
  6. Close the bread with the peanut butter and jelly on it together into a sandwich
